Creative Options Guide

Visual Color Filters

Apply specialized color grading formulas to give each photo a unique vibe:

Cinematic Cool Deep teal shadow tones with warm highlights. Creates a modern high-contrast cinematic film grade.
Energetic Vivid High saturation and boosted contrast. Perfect for action shots, travel, or bright outdoor scenes.
Vintage Warm Warm yellow highlights, desaturated greens, and lowered contrast for a nostalgic film print look.
Noir High-Contrast Dramatic black-and-white grading with deep shadows and bright highlight definitions. Excellent for portraits.
Cyberpunk Neon Intense cyan shadow tinting and magenta highlights. Captures a futuristic neon city aesthetic.
Dreamy Bloom Adds a soft-focus lens glow that bleeds highlights together, creating a dreamy, ethereal fantasy look.
Golden Sunset Applies warm orange/golden hour sepia tones that give photos a warm, radiant feel.
Cool Pine Applies deep forest green tones, desaturates warm hues, and boosts contrast for a moody outdoor look.
Retro Polaroid Fades black levels upwards, desaturates highlights, and wraps the image in a realistic white Polaroid instant border.
Cinematic Letterbox Applies cinematic teal-orange color grading and renders black horizontal letterbox bars on the top and bottom.
Cyberpunk Border Intense cyberpunk neon colors bounded by a sharp glowing cyan cybernetic border frame.
Vintage Grain Warm yellow highlights combined with a dynamic, randomized digital noise texture that simulates organic film grain.
Vaporwave Dream Saturates deep magenta and neon violet tones while adding bright cyan highlight tinting.

Camera Motion & VFX

Control how the camera moves across the image over its display duration:

Slow Zoom In Starts at full frame and slowly scales inwards. Keeps the subject focused and pulls the viewer in.
Slow Zoom Out Starts cropped in and slowly scales outwards. Reveals the surrounding environment of the subject.
Pan Left-to-Right Crops the image and slides the viewport horizontally from left to right. Great for landscape photos.
Pan Right-to-Left Slides the viewport horizontally from right to left, creating a cinematic camera pan.
Tilt Upwards Slides the viewport vertically upwards, mimicking a real camera panning up to scan tall subjects.
Tilt Downwards Slides the viewport vertically downwards to reveal the bottom part of portrait photos.
Slow Spin Applies a slow, subtle rotational camera spin over the duration, keeping the composition dynamic.
Diagonal Pan Pans diagonally from bottom-left to top-right corner while slowly scaling inwards.
Beat Shake Vibrates and shakes the camera viewport dynamically exactly on beat drops to create high-impact hits.
Audio Pulse Continuously scales/zooms the camera in and out in real-time sync with the music amplitude/volume envelope.
Glitch Pulse Triggers rapid digital coordinate offsets and jitters during major beat peaks for an active template edit vibe.
Static (No Motion) Centered still display without any camera motion. Keeps the composition clean and unchanged.

Slide & Blend Transitions

Define how the video transitions from the current clip to the next one:

Crossfade (Blend) The preceding image fades out while the next image fades in underneath, creating a soft cinematic blend.
Slide Left The next image slides in from the right edge, covering the previous image in a swift horizontal wipe.
Slide Right The next image slides in from the left edge towards the right.
Slide Up The next image slides up from the bottom of the screen, creating a clean vertical transition.
Slide Down The next image slides down from the top of the screen.
Slide Diagonal The next image slides in diagonally from the bottom-right corner.
Fade to Black Fades the current clip completely out to black, before fading the next clip back in from black.
Zoom Blend A rapid camera zoom blend transition that pulls the viewport inwards to dissolve into the next clip.
Spin Blend Twists the outgoing frame clockwise while rotating the incoming frame counter-clockwise for a dynamic rotational wipe.
White Flash Flashes a bright white canvas overlay during the transition cut, creating an energetic highlight pop.
Glitch Cut Applies a sudden horizontal chromatic aberration jitter to the next clip's entrance frames for a cybernetic edit.
Cut (None) An instantaneous frame swap. Best for fast-tempo energetic music beats.
